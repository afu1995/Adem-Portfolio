A communication platform for local sport clubs

Communication in sports clubs happens for the most on social media, specially Facebook. Planning training and communicate between different members happens on social media. We wanted to create a project-controlling-system for sports clubs where everybody in the club can communicate internally with each other. The idea was to make it easy for players to see when they have matches and training. It will likewise make it easier for trainers to structure their trainingprogram for the players. The app has a very secure login-function with Hashing, Salted Hashing, Hash Collision and also validation. Admins have the CRUD privileges. They have the functions to Creat, Read, Update and Delete either members, new or events.

We designed and developed the whole app. We used Angular/TypeScript for frontend and Laravel for backend. The database was created with with MySQL.
In frontend we used structural directives such as *ngIf and *ngFor, Router, Nested Routes, Interpolation, Two-way data binding with ngModel and Event binding.
In backend we used Cookies, Migration, Controllers, MVC, Routes and Models.
